Who Are We?

Hi-Fi Electrical is one of the world's leading electrical installation companies for various multi-national corporations. We have been an established business since 2003 starting out as a small-time contractor, in the U.S and growing in both domestic and eventually foreign markets and we have not stopped growing as a company ever since. Our success and reputation built over many years has allowed us the opportunity to pursue different more localised markets.

For instance, recently we have decided to expand our operations into local chain stores across several country's offering a wide variety of services for individuals to take advantage of. This new service will mainly focus on home tv installation however It will also include the installation of broadband and other tasks suited to an electrician.

Of course, in the upcoming months we will be running many more deals for cheaper and better installations as well as several competitions over social media for which the winners will receive custom home cinema setups worth over 6 grand each in total!

At any rate we humbly welcome you to the site. Please feel to browse our many services and we hope that they match your expectations and if they don't please contact us and say so, as we are currently looking for all the feedback we can get.
