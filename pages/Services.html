TV Installation

Our leading service is the installation of various screens form PC Monitor to Smart TVs. The price range differs depending on what service you are requesting, for example installing and setting up a smart tv on its own will not cost more than 80 euro however setting up broadband for the house the price increases thought not substantially. While we would love to list out our entire price range the truth is that there are so many variables that the best way to get a fixed price is to call us and receive a direct quote. You will however find a short general price range in the next few paragraphs from which prices are unlikely to veer far off of.

Tv installation by itself proves relativity cheap at 80 euro, however several factors can change the price rather dramatically. Firstly, whether or not you buying the tv off of us obviously you must pay for the tv but there is also delivery charge of 20 euro to consider. Secondly if the tv is a smart tv than it will probably require a home broadband set up to take full advantage of the sets smart features and while we can install broadband, routers and set it up for you this can increase the cost significantly.

We are also offering to set up home machines whether they be laptops or desktops we can set up both for you however again different tasks will be priced differently according to the variables in play. For example, setting up a conventional laptop straight off of the shelf will be much cheaper than assembling a custom PC gaming rig. Again, please contact us for an exact quote.

Of course, we also deal in maintained and repair of conventional electricians such as lights, fuse boxes and faulty wiring. Again, same rules as before apply prices may vary from situation to situation although no matter what if an electrician does call out to your home you will be charged 30 euro for travel fees.
